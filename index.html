ABOUT BRYAN'S CAFÉ

Bryan's cafe it'ts a chain of coffee shop opened by Bryan Smith, it offers quality product and they are proud of their coffee, that comes directly from Colombia.

There are three branches, and all of them offer the same product and taste guarantee. Although there is one type of coffee that changes every week, the special, it changes in every branch.

HISTORY

HISTORY

Bryan Smith started the company in 1960, with one little coffee shop outside the city. It was outside a shoe factory, early every morning and in the afternoon he would have a lot of customers, the workers of this factory. The word was out, and the delicious flavourful coffee got famous quickly.

As soon as it gained more clientele, he decided to open another shop, but more centric, so he could get customers from the busy offices around the CBD. As it gained more customers it was the moment to open more branches.

So now after so many years, his family business grew and so his family. He now has 2 sons and 1 daugter, each one in charge of each of the branches.
